snake game

by jiro jasmin

let's play to one of the most famous arcade games ever:
the snake game!

the rules are super simple:
you are a moving snake that can't be stopped!
use the arrow keys of your keyboard to control directions and eat the most apples. if you hit the wall or yourself, you loose!

ready ?
click on the start button below!

please note that this game is currently only available on desktop devices, as you need a wide screen and a keyboard to play.
